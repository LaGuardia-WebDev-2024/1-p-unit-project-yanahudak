Your Cat is a Licensed Therapist

Intro

Cats are inteligent, independent, and loving creatures that many people around the world adore. But did you know that owning a cat actually improves your mental health?

Cats provide emotional support to individuals with mental health conditions. Their companionship provides comfort and security, allowing you to cope with stress.

Emotional support for various mental health conditions:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Schizophrenia

How can having a cat reduce stress?

After a long and stressful day, petting your cat can actually reduce your stress from the sound of their purrs. Research shows that the frequency of a cat's purr provides sound therapy to the body.

These vibrations calms the nervous system if you're experiencing symptoms of anxiety such as an increased heart rate and breathing.
